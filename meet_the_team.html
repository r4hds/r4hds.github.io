Meet the Team

Balogun Stephen Taiye

A Medical Doctor and a QI Specialist. Currently rounding up his Master degree programme in Public Health from the prestigious “better by far” university. Had his data science training from the trio of Brian Caffo, Roger Peng and Jeff Leek of the John Hopkins Bloomberg school of Public Health, and his front-end programming with Colleen Van Lent of University of Michigan and the W3CX series.

He is both a fellow and a member of the International Society for Quality in Healthcare (ISQua), a member of the Society for Quality in Health Care in Nigeria (SQHN), and has the HIFA 2016 Country Representative of the year award.
